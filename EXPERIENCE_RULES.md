# EXPERIENCE RULES

## Core Principle

A premium product is not only seen.

It is felt.

Motion, reveal timing, transitions, feedback and rhythm define perception.

The experience must feel alive, intentional and controlled.

---

## Global Rules

Every ecosystem product must:

- reveal progressively
- avoid instant information overload
- guide the eye naturally
- reward interaction subtly
- feel smooth but disciplined

Never static.
Never chaotic.

---

## Page Entry Rules

Page loads must feel staged.

Order:

1. Hero appears
2. Primary metrics reveal
3. Charts animate
4. Secondary content fades in

Staggered.

Never everything at once.

---

## Dashboard Behavior

Dashboard must feel:

alive.

Rules:

- hero fades + rises
- metrics stagger
- charts draw progressively
- active values pulse softly
- updates feel responsive

---

## Sidebar Behavior

Sidebar must feel:

stable.

Rules:

- collapse softly
- active state glows subtly
- hover clarifies, never jumps
- transitions are minimal

Sidebar is anchor.

---

## Card Behavior

Cards must feel:

reactive.

Allowed:

- subtle lift
- border brighten
- halo glow
- soft reveal

Never aggressive.

---

## Chart Behavior

Charts must feel:

premium intelligence.

Rules:

- draw from origin
- smooth transitions
- data focus
- no visual noise
- hover reveals detail cleanly

Charts are storytelling objects.

---

## VOLTIS Experience

Voltis motion should feel:

electric
fast
sharp
high tension

Keywords:

spark
scan
pulse
strike
fracture

---

## VITALIS Experience

Vitalis motion should feel:

breathing
organic
cyclic
warm
alive

Keywords:

grow
pulse
expand
settle
breathe

---

## Final Test

Ask:

Does the product feel alive?

If not:
the experience is incomplete.
