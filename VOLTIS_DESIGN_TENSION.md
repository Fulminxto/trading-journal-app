# VOLTIS_DESIGN_TENSION.md (v1)

## Core Principle

VOLTIS is not designed to impress immediately.

It is designed to reveal authority over time.

The system must feel:

-   private
-   rare
-   sharp
-   cold
-   intelligent
-   precise
-   alive

Never:

-   generic
-   overdesigned
-   decorative
-   predictable
-   crowded
-   soft

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Emotional Architecture

Every page must carry a distinct psychological role.

Not just a function.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Dashboard

Identity: Authority Calm Emotion: "I know exactly what is happening."

Must feel: - dominant - stable - silent - controlled

Must avoid: - excitement - noise - over-density

Signature mechanics: - dominant hero chart - minimal metrics - wide
breathing space - slow transitions - live-point subtle pulse only

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Diary

Identity: Surgical Timeline Emotion: "Every trade is evidence."

Must feel: - procedural - sequential - precise

Must avoid: - spreadsheet feeling - generic tables

Signature mechanics: - timeline continuity - thin operational rows -
filter bar as toolbelt - replay entry as continuation

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Calendar

Identity: Temporal Discipline Emotion: "I can see my discipline across
time."

Must feel: - historical - cumulative - behavioral

Must avoid: - aggressive heatmap behavior - loud hover effects

Signature mechanics: - subtle day focus - chapter-based month
navigation - temporal rhythm

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Equity

Identity: Controlled Growth Emotion: "This capital survives."

Must feel: - measured - resilient - protected

Must avoid: - casino excitement - aggressive green glow

Signature mechanics: - clean progression line - underwater curve
prominence - point-only rendering on single trade

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Analytics

Identity: Brutal Truth Engine Emotion: "I cannot lie here."

Must feel: - clinical - dense - unforgiving - revealing

Must avoid: - dashboard duplication - decorative softness

Signature mechanics: - tighter density - harsh truth emphasis -
behavioral risk stronger than vanity metrics

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Reports

Identity: Performance Tribunal Emotion: "This documents who I am
becoming."

Must feel: - official - archivable - heavy - structured

Must avoid: - live-dashboard energy - too much motion

Signature mechanics: - chapter rhythm - strong typography - export
prominence - reading-first structure

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Copilot

Identity: Strategic Mirror Emotion: "This sees through me."

Must feel: - intimate - intelligent - confrontational - reflective

Must avoid: - chatbot energy - customer support feeling

Signature mechanics: - compressed memory - scroll-contained
conversation - limited visible history - heavy prompts - strong
psychological warnings

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# System-wide Tension Rules

## Controlled Asymmetry

Allowed hero types: - hero-chart - hero-command - hero-report -
hero-control - hero-identity - hero-dossier - hero-doctrine

## Signature Moments

Every page must contain one memorable mechanic. Only one.

## Compression Rhythm

Fixed order: Header → ScopeBar → Hero → Tools → Content → Secondary

## Highlight Discipline

Glow only where tension matters.

## Semantic Purity

Green/red/yellow only for: - profit - loss - risk - warning

Never for identity.

## Information Density Scaling

-   Dashboard → light
-   Diary → medium
-   Calendar → medium
-   Equity → medium-light
-   Analytics → high
-   Reports → medium-heavy
-   Copilot → low-heavy
